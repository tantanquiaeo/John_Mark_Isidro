About

Greetings citizens of earth, I’ve come in peace. I am John Mark Isidro, a creature who emerged on the 20th of February in the year 2002 at Culiong Salcedo, Ilocos Sur. I am a big fan of music. I like singing but it seems that singing doesn’t like me (I still sing a lot though hihi). I do also like writing; it allows me express things that I usually can’t talk about verbally. It’s an escape for me. But I’ve lost it that’s why I found a new hobby which is sleeping. Sleeping severs my ties with reality for a short while. Its an escape and peace at the same time for a limited period. When I’m asleep, it’s the quietest my mind is in a day, that’s why I treasure sleep. “Come what may.” I consider that as my philosophy in life. Because I am a firm believer that wherever I am is where I should be. I do believe in his plan. Disappointments and failure are all part of his plan so as victories and success.

If you’re asking me, "where do I see myself 10 years from now?", I wouldn’t be able to answer it. Because in life, nothing is certain or definite. Possibilities do come and go everyday, creating our "what ifs" in life. Let me alter the question a bit “where do I want myself to be 10 years from now”, then that I can answer. I picture myself sitting outside on the balcony of a house I’ve built with my own money in the barangay where I was born, sipping a cup of coffee while I watch 7 of my kids (4 boys and 3 girls) play on the backyard while their mother is shouting and telling them to stop running around. Aside from that I also want to own a coffee shop (with free wifi of course). It will have a rustic vibe and a retro concept. I also want be able to travel wherever I want, whenever I want. As a teacher? I want to be someone who is a second parent to his students. I don’t want to be just their teacher. I want them to trust me as if I’m their friend. Because that’s how my teachers seemed to me during my elementary and secondary days. And my ultimate goal? To reach the stability where when I go buy groceries, I just put items in my cart without checking the price. To end this introduction, I’ll leave everyone crazy enough to read this a quote I made: “Don’t limit yourself; go dream, the more entries you have the more chances of winning.”
